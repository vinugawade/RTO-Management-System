Our organization

We are a governmental organization and we operate in all the districts of Maharashtra We have skilled officers and inspectors who smoothly run the organization

The things we do are

  • Issue driving license
  • Issue learner's license
  • Issue vehicle registration certificate
  • Amend traffic rules and regulations
  • Conduct awareness programmes on road safety, pollution control etc.

About Us

Organisation of the Indian government responsible for maintaining a database of drivers and a database of vehicles for Maharashtra. It issues driving licences, organises collection of vehicle excise duty and sells personalised registrations. It also is responsible to inspect vehicle's insurance and clear the pollution test.
